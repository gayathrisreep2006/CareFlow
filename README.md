# 🏥 CareFlow

An AI-powered patient appointment and waiting time management system developed as a prototype for the **Israel–India Hackathon 2026**.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

**Patient Appointment and Waiting Time Mismanagement**

Many hospitals face challenges in managing patient appointments and waiting times efficiently, resulting in long queues, scheduling conflicts, and reduced patient satisfaction.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

CareFlow is designed to:

* Streamline patient appointment scheduling
* Reduce waiting times through smart queue management
* Provide AI-assisted patient support
* Improve hospital workflow and patient experience
* Visualize appointment data through an interactive dashboard
